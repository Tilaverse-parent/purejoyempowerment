Our Story

Where Pure Joy Began

Pure Joy Empowerment Uganda (PJWEU) was founded in 2018 in Jinja City out of a deep calling to serve the most vulnerable — street children sleeping in markets, women with no income and no path forward, and boys growing up without positive male role models.

What started as a small tailoring and hairdressing training centre has evolved into a Women-Led Circular Economy and Regenerative Agrocultural Enterprise Hub — serving Jinja City, Jinja District, Buikwe, and Kayunga across Eastern Uganda.

Our organisation and philosophy is rooted in Christian values and the love of God — believing that every person is made in God's image and deserves dignity, opportunity, and hope.
