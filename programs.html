Program 7

Clean Energy Initiative

Our clean energy pillar produces renewable biofuels and locally manufactured cooking stoves — reducing deforestation, cutting indoor air pollution, and creating new income streams for women and youth.

🌿

Biodiesel Production

Biodiesel from Jatropha, Castor, Moringa, Croton & CNSL for vehicles, generators, and kerosene stove replacement.

⚡

Bioethanol from Sorghum

Sweet Sorghum-derived bioethanol for clean cooking stoves, liquid fuel lamps, and small engine power — drought-resistant and food-safe.

🔥

Waste-to-Briquettes

Converting pineapple waste (Twiga Ananasi), cashew shells, and crushed sorghum sticks into low-cost cooking briquettes replacing charcoal.
