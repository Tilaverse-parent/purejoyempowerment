Donations & Giving
Visit our Donate page to give securely online using a credit or debit card. You can choose a one-time gift, a monthly recurring donation, or an annual gift. We accept all major cards through Stripe, a trusted, PCI DSS-certified payment processor. For bank transfers or other methods, email donations@purejoyempowerment.org.
Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe, Inc., which is certified to PCI DSS Level 1 — the highest standard for payment security. Pure Joy never stores or sees your full card number, CVV, or bank details. All data is transmitted via 256-bit HTTPS encryption.
100% of your donation goes directly to our programmes in Uganda, covering:
  • Housing, food, and care for children in our home
  • School fees, uniforms, books, and tutoring
  • Women's vocational training and enterprise development
  • Clean energy equipment manufacturing
  • Nutrition and feeding programmes
  • Boys mentorship and street children outreach
We publish annual impact reports for all donors who request them.
Tax deductibility depends on your country of residence and applicable local tax law. We recommend consulting a tax adviser in your jurisdiction. We issue donation receipts via email upon request. Austrian donors giving through our Caritas Austria administrative channel may have additional options — please contact us for details.
Yes — you can cancel or modify a recurring (monthly or annual) donation at any time. Simply email donations@purejoyempowerment.org at least 5 business days before your next billing date. We'll confirm the cancellation by return email.
Our online donation form currently processes payments in USD. However, Stripe automatically handles the currency conversion from your card's native currency, so you can donate from anywhere in the world. For Euro-denominated donations through Caritas Austria, please contact us.
All donations are final. Refunds are only issued in cases of clear processing errors (e.g., duplicate charge or incorrect amount). Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of the transaction to donations@purejoyempowerment.org.
Child Sponsorship
Our child sponsorship programme connects generous donors with a specific child in our care. Your monthly gift covers that child's housing, meals, school fees, healthcare, and emotional support. You will receive a welcome pack with your sponsored child's story, and quarterly photo and progress updates. Visit our Sponsor a Child page to begin.
We offer three tiers: Seed ($25/month) — meals and basic supplies; Joy ($50/month) — full care including school fees and healthcare; Champion ($100/month) — full care plus vocational training contribution and a quarterly impact report. All tiers make a meaningful difference.
Yes. Sponsors receive: a welcome pack with their child's background and photo; school progress updates each term; a birthday message from the child; and an annual report. Champion-tier sponsors receive a quarterly written update. All updates are shared with appropriate consent and child safeguarding considerations.
Volunteering
Complete the expression-of-interest form on our Get Involved page. You can also apply through our volunteer partners: SenseMacres and Auslandsdienst Austria. We aim to respond to all applications within 5 business days.
We actively seek volunteers with expertise in:
  • Regenerative and organic agriculture / agronomy
  • Textile design and garment production
  • Cosmetic chemistry and natural beauty products
  • Renewable energy engineering (bioethanol, biodiesel, briquettes)
  • Social enterprise strategy and business development
  • Digital marketing, content creation, and fundraising
  • Education and teaching (primary / secondary level)
Placements typically run 3–12 months. Shorter engagements may be considered for specialist consultants. We find that placements of at least 3 months allow meaningful integration and measurable impact in our programmes.
Yes. All international volunteers require a valid Ugandan volunteer or work permit. We provide guidance on the application process and documentation required. Caritas Austria administers permit support for Austrian volunteers; other nationalities should contact us early as processing takes 4–8 weeks.
We provide accommodation support and guidance for long-term volunteers, including connections to trusted local housing near our centre in Jinja City. Accommodation costs and living expenses are the volunteer's responsibility. We provide a detailed pre-arrival briefing covering costs, healthcare, safety, and practical logistics.
Yes — we actively welcome students from universities of applied sciences and similar institutions. We are currently formalising partnerships with European universities for structured student placements. Please contact us with your university, course of study, and desired placement period. We can work with your institution's placement office to establish a formal agreement.
Our Programmes
We operate across four areas of Eastern Uganda: Jinja City, Jinja District, Buikwe, and Kayunga. Our main centre and children's home is located in Jinja City.
Our children's home currently houses over 60 children — orphaned, street-connected, or at-risk youth — in a safe, family-style environment. 95% of children in our care are enrolled in formal schooling. For more information, visit our Orphanage page.
Pure Joy is transitioning from traditional vocational training into a Women-Led Circular Economy and Regenerative Agrocultural Enterprise Hub. This means linking skills training directly to sustainable market enterprises — biofuels, banana fibre products, natural beauty, clean energy stoves, and school food systems — creating lasting income and environmental resilience for communities in Eastern Uganda. We are not replacing Pure Joy; we are upgrading it.
Our partners include Amelia Agro, Twiga Sun Fruits, Busoga Flower Food Company, Maridadi Beauty Care, Saints General Workshop, Bulikimu Tusobola, SenseMacres, Auslandsdienst Austria, and Caritas Austria. See our Partners page for full details.
