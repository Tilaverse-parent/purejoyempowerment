About Pure Joy

From Skills to
Sustainable Enterprise

Pure Joy Empowerment Uganda (PJWEU) is a Christian-values-driven NGO transitioning from traditional vocational training into a Women-Led Circular Economy and Regenerative Agrocultural Enterprise Hub in Eastern Uganda.

We serve street children, orphans, women, and youth across Jinja City, Jinja District, Buikwe, and Kayunga — equipping every person with dignity, skills, and a sustainable livelihood.

Safe Haven

The Pure Joy Children's Home

Our children's home is a place of warmth, safety, and unconditional love — a refuge for orphaned and street-connected children who deserve a second chance at life.

Safe, clean, and loving accommodation for every child
Daily nutritious, home-cooked meals and healthcare
Access to quality formal schooling & tutoring
Emotional, spiritual, and psychological support
Long-term vocational pathways for older youth
