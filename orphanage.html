The Pure Joy Children's Home

Est.2018
Safe Haven

A Home Built on Love & Hope

Every child in our home was once sleeping in markets, doorways, or unsafe streets. The Pure Joy Children's Home is a place of unconditional love — safe, warm, and full of opportunity.

We provide long-term, family-style care: not just a roof over each child's head, but a complete support system of education, nutrition, healthcare, emotional support, and spiritual formation.

Safe, clean, and loving accommodation
Daily nutritious, home-cooked meals
Formal school enrolment and academic tutoring
Healthcare, dental, and psychological support
Spiritual formation and Christian values
Vocational pathway preparation for older youth
