Pure Joy Empowerment Uganda ("PJWEU", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you visit purejoyempowerment.org, make a donation, apply to volunteer, or otherwise interact with us. Please read it carefully.

1. Who We Are

Pure Joy Empowerment Uganda (PJWEU) is a registered non-governmental organisation based in Jinja City, Uganda (Registration: PJWEU/2018/JJA), serving Jinja City, Jinja District, Buikwe, and Kayunga. Administrative oversight for our international volunteer programme is provided by Caritas Austria.

Data enquiries: info@purejoyempowerment.org

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Donations: name, email, and payment details (processed by Stripe — we never store card numbers)
  • Contact forms: name, email, phone, subject, and message
  • Volunteer applications: name, email, skill area, and availability
  • Newsletter sign-ups: email address only
  • Partnership enquiries: organisation name, contact name, and email

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address and approximate location (country/city)
  • Browser type, device, and operating system
  • Pages visited, navigation paths, and session duration
  • Referring source or URL

2.3 Information from Partners

  • Volunteer referrals from SenseMacres, Auslandsdienst Austria, or Caritas Austria may include basic contact information for prospective volunteers
  • Payment confirmation data from Stripe (transaction ID, amount, status)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process donations and issue receipts
  • Respond to enquiries and support requests
  • Process and evaluate volunteer applications
  • Send newsletters and impact updates (only with your consent)
  • Maintain accurate donor, supporter, and volunteer records
  • Comply with legal, accounting, and regulatory obligations
  • Improve our website, programmes, and communications
  • Detect and prevent fraud or misuse

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for commercial purposes, and we do not use your data for automated profiling or decision-making.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For individuals in the EEA or UK, we process personal data under the following bases:

  • Contract performance — processing donations, volunteer agreements, and service requests
  • Legitimate interests — donor communications, fraud prevention, service improvement
  • Consent — newsletters (withdrawable at any time via unsubscribe link)
  • Legal obligation — accounting, tax reporting, audit compliance

5. Who We Share Data With

  • Stripe Inc. — payment processing (PCI DSS compliant)
  • Caritas Austria — volunteer programme administration (under data processing agreement)
  • Email service providers — receipts, newsletters, and communications
  • Vercel / Netlify — website hosting infrastructure
  • Legal authorities — when required by law or to protect legal rights

All processors are bound by data processing agreements and may only act on our documented instructions.

6. Donation & Payment Data

All donations are processed by Stripe, Inc. (PCI DSS Level 1 certified). We never store or access your full card number, CVV, or bank details. Stripe provides us with: your name, email, transaction ID, date, amount, and payment status. Donation records are retained for 7 years for accounting and audit purposes. Receipts are sent to your provided email address.

7. Cookies & Tracking

  • Essential cookies: Required for site functionality. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: If analytics is enabled, aggregated visitor data (anonymised) helps us improve the site. You may disable these via browser settings without any loss of functionality.

We do not use advertising or retargeting cookies and do not share data with advertising networks.

8. How Long We Keep Data

  • Donation records: 7 years (legal obligation)
  • Contact form submissions: 2 years from last contact
  • Volunteer records: 2 years after placement ends
  • Newsletter subscribers: Until you unsubscribe
  • Analytics data: 26 months (anonymised)

9. Your Rights

You have the right to: access your data · correct inaccuracies · delete your data (subject to legal retention) · restrict processing · data portability · object to processing · withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any right, email info@purejoyempowerment.org. We will respond within 30 days. You may also lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.

10. International Transfers

Our service providers (Stripe, Vercel/Netlify, email platforms) may be based in the US or EU. All international transfers use Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent safeguards approved under applicable data protection law.

11. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from under-16s without verifiable parental consent. Photographs of children in our programmes are taken only with guardian consent, and no beneficiary child is publicly identified without appropriate authorisation and safeguarding review.

12. Security

We protect your data using HTTPS encryption, PCI DSS-compliant payment processing, access controls, and regular security reviews. No internet system is 100% secure; in the event of a breach posing material risk, we will notify you and relevant authorities as legally required.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above and, where appropriate, communicated by email. Continued use of our website constitutes acceptance of any updated policy.
